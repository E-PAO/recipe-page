Simple Omelette Page

Prep Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or vegetable oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, meat, herbs, etc.

Instructions

1.

Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are mixed. Add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

2.

Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

3.

Prepare the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

4.

Add fillings(optional): As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

5.

Enjoy!: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
